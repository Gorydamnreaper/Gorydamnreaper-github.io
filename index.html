Pathfinder Adventure: Seasons of Ghosts

Something strange is happening to your hometown of Willowshore! Nestled on the banks of a river winding through the legendary Specterwood in Tian Xia’s haunted land of Shenmen, the people of Willowshore are no strangers to supernatural threat, but the danger that comes to town on the first day of summer is unlike anything you’ve ever seen before. Over the four seasons to come, you and your fellow home-grown heroes must face evil spirits, sinister fiends, and frightening curses, lest the town of Willowshore succumb to the Season of Ghosts!

The Story So Far

Once upon a time, in the tranquil town of Willowshore, nestled in the haunted land of Shenmen, a peculiar mystery began to unfold. It was a place where whispers of spirits and shadows of the past were as common as the rustling of leaves. Yet, the arrival of summer brought with it an unsettling change, one that even the most seasoned inhabitants had never seen before. As the sun cast its first summer rays, a creeping mist enveloped the town, and with it, tales of eerie apparitions and malevolent beings began to surface. Willowshore, cradled by the enigmatic Specterwood, found itself at the heart of a supernatural storm. From the depths of the forest came whispers of curses and sightings of fiendish creatures, shattering the fragile peace of the town. In these trying times, a band of heroes rose from among the townsfolk—individuals of courage, wisdom, and heart. They ventured into the shadowy woods, facing specters and sinister forces, driven by a singular purpose: to unravel the mysteries of the Season of Ghosts. Their journey is not just a battle against otherworldly foes but a quest for understanding, where every encounter is a step toward discovering the truth that binds Willowshore to its spectral visitors. This is their story, a tale of bravery against the unknown, of bonds forged in the crucible of fear and hope. It is a reminder that even in the darkest of times, the light of human spirit and camaraderie can guide us through the shadows.
